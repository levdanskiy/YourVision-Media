# 📈 STYLE EVOLUTION LOG (2026)

## 📅 UPDATE: 06.02.2026 (Trend Calibration)

### 1. VISUAL CORE (AI & Photography)
- **Status:** Transitioning from "AI Polish" to "Organic Realism".
- **Key Directive:** Implement **"Imperfect by Design"**. Use `--style raw` and include descriptions of film grain, motion blur, and non-centered compositions.
- **Color Palette:** Moving towards glow gradients and "Atmospheric Depth".

### 2. TONE OF VOICE (ToV)
- **Standard:** "Confident Restraint". Less visual noise, more narrative power.
- **YV:** Fast-paced but vulnerable storytelling.
- **AC/NB:** Lyrical minimalism. Avoid "Visual Noise".

### 3. TELEGRAM LAYOUT
- **Focus:** Narrative organizations. Media must tell a story, not just illustrate text. Use bold headlines and strategic white space.
